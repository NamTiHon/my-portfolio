National Exam RAG Chatbot & Dashboard

Data Analytics GenAI

Project Overview

This project focuses on developing an interactive website to visualize and analyze the National High School Graduation Exam scores in 2023 in VietNam. The final product is an interactive web platform where users, including students and educators, can explore academic trends, analyze scores by subject and admission block, and even interact with an AI Chatbot to get data-driven answers.

My Role & Responsibilities

Role: Data Analyst

  • Data Visualization: Visualized the national score data using Python libraries (Matplotlib, Seaborn) to create distribution plots, pie charts, and correlation matrices.
  • Analytical Storytelling: Interpreted the score trends, uncovered key anomalies, stories, and academic insights hidden within the raw graduation numbers.
  • Dashboard Narrative: Structured the analytical insights, tables, and graphs into a logical narrative, guiding dashboard users from a national overview down to subject-level analyses.
